Is this item worth $15+ if sold?

💡 Quick Tip

Consider shipping costs and time involved. Only mark "Yes" if you'd net $15+ after all expenses.

Is this a sentimental/inherited item?
Is this item rare, hard to replace, or truly exceptional quality?

🚩 Red Flags to Say "No"

  • "I might need this someday" (but no specific plan)
  • Duplicate of something you already use
  • Doesn't fit your current lifestyle
Does this item reflect who I am now or who I'm becoming?

✅ Green Flags to Say "Yes"

  • Actively brings joy when you see it
  • You've thought about it while it was packed
  • Reflects your authentic self
Does holding this item make me feel positive emotions I want to carry forward?
Can I display this meaningfully when I get proper furniture?
Is this the only/best item I have from this person?
Could I use this item in daily life to honor the memory?
